✅ Rate Calculation Formula:
   rate = (coherent validations / total validations) × 100
   where coherent = deviation < 1%

✅ Rate Range:
   Always between 0 and 100 (inclusive)
   Never NaN or infinite

✅ High Coherence:
   Notes with deviations < 1% achieve rate >= 95%
   No critical alerts emitted

✅ Critical Alert:
   Emitted when rate < 95%
   Contains rate, threshold, and validation counts

✅ Monotonicity:
   Improving note coherence doesn't decrease global rate

✅ Alert Details:
   Level: 'critical'
   Message: mentions rate and 95% threshold
   Details: exact rate, coherent count, total count
